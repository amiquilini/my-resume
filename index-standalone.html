Who I Am

Backend engineer with 4+ years of experience building distributed systems and cloud-native solutions. I specialize in .NET and AWS, with a focus on microservices architecture, event-driven design, and legacy system modernization.

My background in Computational Biochemistry and Chemistry — with research at UNICAMP and an exchange year at Columbia University — shaped how I approach complex problems: analytically, methodically, and with genuine curiosity for understanding systems at a deep level. That scientific foundation translates directly into engineering discipline.

Currently working at IBM on a large-scale banking project (Itaú), where I design and maintain mission-critical systems under strict compliance and observability standards.
